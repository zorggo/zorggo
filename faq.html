What is Zorggo?

Zorggo is a smart alternative to the traditional two wheeler workshop, we provide two wheeler services at your doorstep, helping you to save your time and money.

How long does a service take?

Service time is based on your vehicle condition, but it usally take approximately 60-90 minutes.

Do you require a big space and what type of space for the service?

We don't require a big space for service, probably your two wheeler parking should be fine. We request the customers to provide a space where there no animal intervention and kids too, for avoiding any accidents.

How can I pay for the service?

You can pay through online or you can pay cash just after the service, then you will get final bill on your email.

Can I book a service on any day?

Yes. You can book service on any day. But, you cannot book the service for the same day. The service slots will be available only one day pre order.

Can i change my address after booking the service?

Yes. You can update the details on our mobile application, before 6 PM on the day prior to the date of the service.

How do I cancel the service appointment which i have booked?

You can cancel the booking within 2 hours after the booking.

Can i place a service request for additional two wheeler when the technician is at my place?

Yes. It is possible when your additional two wheeler available on same address.
